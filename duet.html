Duet POS User Interface

Duet is a tablet point of sale system developed by ASI that draws on the company's 30 years of restaurant industry experience. I worked with the Product Manager on design elements and concepts to help create a user friendly and easy to learn experience. The software is used by multiple levels of restaurant staff who have a variety of expectations and needs for the software.

The UI needed to be clean and simple due to the high volume and fast paced environment the software is used in. Blue from the product logo works to tie screens together while a strong use of white space helps the user to easily find their next action and smoothly flow from one screen to the next.
