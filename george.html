Ad for Ron George

I created an ad for Ron George who ran for govenor of Maryland in 2014. This ad was used by Ron George's campaign to oppose a rain tax for Maryland residents. I utilized red and blue as the main colors for the political connotations. The blue background was a stiking backdrop to make the umbrella stand out. The tagline follows the arc of the umbrella, unifying the imagery and text. The ad was used in print and in a campaign video.
